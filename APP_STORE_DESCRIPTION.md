Short Description:
Display your parkrun barcode during activities - no need to stop your watch!

Full Description:
Show your parkrun barcode as a QR code directly on your Garmin watch, without having to end or exit your activity. Perfect for scanning at the finish line while your run keeps recording.

Setup Instructions:

1. Install this data field from the Connect IQ Store

2. Set your parkrun ID in the Garmin Connect app:
   - Go to the data field settings
   - Enter your parkrun athlete ID (e.g., A1234567)

3. Add a data field screen to your activity:
   - On your watch, go to the activity (e.g., "Run")
   - Edit the data screens
   - Add a new screen with a single data field layout
   - Select this QR Code data field

Important:
Use single field layout only - The QR code must be displayed full-screen to be large enough for reliable scanning. Multi-field layouts will make the code too small.

Usage:
During your parkrun, when you reach the finish funnel:
1. Pause or stop your activity
2. Press the back/lap button to access your data screens
3. Scroll to the QR code screen

Volunteers can scan directly from your watch while your activity stays recorded!
